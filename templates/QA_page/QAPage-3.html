  • A Supplementary Tool to PubMed

    The IQS provides you functions PubMed doesn't provide, allowing you to search related literature by articles, dig into the related articles with your collaborators (coming soon), and find research inspirations using the unique query mode functions available.

  • To Find Out Inspirations

    • Please use the filter on the left panel to include or exclude the pre-prints.
    • Categorize pertinent articles in different repositories. 
    • Save or share the articles by repository.

  • To Read Main Stream Articles

    • Please choose "High Citation Articles" on the left panel to show only mainstream articles. 
    • Save them to the repository. 
    • Cancel the "High Citation Articles" choice to compare the searching outcomes.
